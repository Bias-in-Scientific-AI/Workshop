Bias in Scientific AI

A four-day workshop bringing together researchers, practitioners, and community partners to examine how bias enters scientific AI systems and how scientific communities can build more reliable, inclusive methods.

Register interest View program
Date 12-15 April 2027
Location University of Sao Paulo, Brazil
Funded by Schmidt Sciences Community Initiative Fund

Workshop Focus

Scientific AI now shapes discovery across disciplines. The workshop is designed to turn shared concern about bias into practical methods, community standards, and collaborations.

Methods

Detecting bias in scientific AI

Sessions will examine data, models, benchmarks, evaluation practices, and scientific workflows where bias can distort research outcomes.

Community

Building shared practice

The program will support discussion across fields and geographies, with emphasis on practical guidance that communities can adopt.

Outcomes

From workshop to action

Participants will work toward reusable resources, collaborations, and recommendations for responsible scientific AI development.
